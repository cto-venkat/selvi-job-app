## 2. UK University Hiring Process

### Timeline
- Internal approval: 2-8 weeks
- Advert live: 3-4 weeks
- Shortlisting: 1-2 weeks after close
- Interview day: single "assessment day" (presentation + panel + teaching demo)
- Offer: 1-2 weeks post-interview
- Start: aligned to semester (Sept or Jan)
- Total: 3-6 months

### Hidden Market
- Fractional/fixed-term to permanent pipeline is the main entry point
- Internal candidates often known before posting (advert is formality)
- Conference networking (SRHE, BERA) is how candidates become known
- JISCMail discipline lists carry informal "looking for someone" messages
- External examiner networks create hiring relationships

### REF Impact
- REF2029 upcoming -- universities hiring people with strong publication records
- Teaching-only contracts growing (Teaching Fellow, Lecturer (Education))
- "Impact case studies" value real-world L&D practice
- Post-92 universities more teaching-focused = less competitive for this profile
